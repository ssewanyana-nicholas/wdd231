Overview

Purpose

The website will serve as an online hub for the Uganda Golf Club to showcase its golfing facilities, promote events and tournaments, enable membership sign-ups, and provide updates for members and visitors. It will also highlight the clubâ€™s rich history, location, and services such as the clubhouse, pro shop, and training programs.

Audience

Target Audience:

  • Golf enthusiasts (local and international)
  • Corporate professionals looking for networking venues
  • Tourists seeking premium leisure experiences
  • Local families and elites interested in club membership
  • Youth and beginner golfers interested in learning

Needs & Expectations:
Visitors want to explore membership options, book tee times, view tournament schedules, and find contact/location info easily. The site must be mobile and desktop-friendly to accommodate a broad range of users.
